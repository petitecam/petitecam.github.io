Who is Jul?

Julien Marie (Jul) was born on January 14, 1990 in Marseille. He lived throughout his youth in Saint Jean du Désert, for a few years he has lived in Cassis. Jul is a role model for everyone, he worked very hard to get where he is now. He is the first salesman of French rap ever known. He left school in ninth grade and built swimming pools with his father. Since the beginning of his college Jul rapped in his room (he made his own beats, wrote his texts and rapped before recording). He broke into music with the sound Sort le cross volé released in 2014.
